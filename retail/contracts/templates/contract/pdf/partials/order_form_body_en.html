Conversation definition: A continuous exchange of inbound messages (from the customer) or outbound messages (from an agent or system), subject to two rules:

  • Daily reset: the conversation is measured within a calendar day (00:00–23:59). Interactions that cross midnight count as two conversations.
  • Per channel: the conversation is tied to the originating channel. Migration between channels (e.g., Webchat → WhatsApp) is counted as two separate conversations.
